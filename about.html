    About Us

    Who We Are !

    THIDA AUTO CENTER is a US based company and under the THIDA CONSULTING GROUP LLC.

    What We Do !

    We export all kinds of New and Used cars from US, Canada and Mexico, and we export worldwide. When it comes to import a car, we are #1 source for quality American, Canada, and Mexico cars.

    We help worldwide buyers to buy new and used cars for sale and export them to any country in the world. As a professional car trader, we provide quality cars’ door to port services for each car we sold to you.

    We offer USA, Canada, and Mexico cars for export to many countries: Australia, New Zealand, Middle East, South America, Europe, Africa, and Asia.

    Our Goal !

    Our goal is to build a long term mutual beneficial relationship with our clients by providing dedication, reliability, confidence, and peaceful buying experience to all the product and service we supply to every client.
